WHY DO WE NEED TO THINK ABOUT THE FUTURE OF TRANSPORTION IN BIG CITIES?

In Budapest and all major European cities, car traffic has more than doubled in the past twenty years. Cities cannot accommodate more cars. In order for cities to remain livable, car traffic must be reduced, and public transportation must be improved.
